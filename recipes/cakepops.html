Cake Pops!

Cake pops... for Pops?

Tasty, easy-making, and perfect for kids.

Ingredients

  • Cake
  • Popcicle sticks
  • Baking chocolate

Steps

  1. Make cake.
  2. Break up cake. Press into spheres.
  3. Insert Popcicle stick. Dip in chocolate.
  4. Let chocolate harden on baking sheet.
  5. Enjoy!
