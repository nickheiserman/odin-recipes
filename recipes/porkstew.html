Pork Stew

Pork Stew... Dork Stew:

A tomato and vegetable pork stew that is sure to please any crowd! It is a long cooking process, good for a day when you are staying at/working at home, but WELL worth the invested time.

Ingredients

  • Ground pork
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Kale
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dry chilies
  • Potatos
  • Stock (your preference)

Steps

  1. Brown the ground pork with onion and garlic. Rehidrate the dried chilies in water on a low simmer for 15 minutes. Water should barely cover the chilies.
  2. Drain chilies and pure.
  3. Dice potatoes. Add potatos, pork, and chilies to pot with 4 cups of stock.
  4. Salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Turn off heat, add chopped kale.
