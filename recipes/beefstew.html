Beef Stew

Beef Stew... for two:

Rutabagas and beef chuck roast make this stew a hearty and comforting main dish any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • Roast Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Kale
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dry chilies
  • Rutabega
  • Stock (your preference)

Steps

  1. Brown the beef with onion and garlic. Rehidrate the dried chilies in water on a low simmer for 15 minutes. Water should barely cover the chilies.
  2. Drain chilies and pure.
  3. Dice rutabegas. Add rutabegas, pork, and chilies to pot with 4 cups of stock.
  4. Salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Turn off heat, add chopped kale.
